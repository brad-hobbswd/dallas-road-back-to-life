Chapter Five

The Mountain of Freedom

Freedom often waits on the other side of surrender.

The trail rose sharply, winding toward a mountain that seemed to pierce the sky. Dallas looked up at the peak, chest tightening with anticipation. The climb ahead was steep, rocky, and uncertain.

Every muscle in his legs ached already, yet he knew this mountain represented more than a physical challenge. This was the place where the deepest battles within him would surface.

He paused at the base and remembered the words of Solomon and Micah. Temptation, fear, doubt. Those voices would still exist. God's presence would also remain, steadfast and powerful.

Dallas bowed his head and whispered:

"Lord, I want to climb this mountain with You."
