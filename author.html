About the Author

Bradley Hobbs writes with a deep conviction that Jesus still heals wounded hearts, restores broken places, and reaches into stories that many believe are beyond redemption. His writing grows from a desire to speak hope into places of pain and to remind readers that no life is too fractured for the mercy of God to reach. Through devotionals, reflections, and faith centered storytelling, he seeks to create spaces where people feel seen, understood, and encouraged to continue walking forward.

His years serving in teaching and child development provided firsthand experiences that shaped both his compassion and perspective. During those years he witnessed how deeply words can wound and how powerfully encouragement, truth, and love can help people rise again. Those experiences continue to influence the heart behind every page he writes.

Personal seasons of spiritual wrestling, moments of misunderstanding, and experiences of feeling unseen have also shaped his voice. Rather than allowing difficult seasons to produce silence, those experiences became part of a calling to reach people who quietly carry burdens of shame, grief, rejection, and hidden struggles. His work remains rooted in the Oneness revelation that God is one and His name is Jesus.

Every book and devotional carries a simple prayer. Bradley hopes readers will discover healing, renewed faith, and the assurance that Jesus never abandons the people He loves. His desire is that every person who encounters his writing will leave strengthened, encouraged, and reminded that hope remains alive even in the middle of difficult seasons.
