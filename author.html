About the Author

Bradley Hobbs writes with the conviction that Jesus still heals wounded hearts and reaches into stories that seem impossible to redeem. He served six years in teaching and child development, where he observed firsthand how the human spirit bends under pressure yet finds remarkable ways to rise when encouraged by love and truth.

His life experiences, including seasons of spiritual wrestling, moments of being misunderstood, and a deep desire to help those who struggle silently, have shaped his voice and mission. His devotional work is rooted in the Oneness revelation that God is one and His name is Jesus.

Every page he writes seeks to lift the broken, strengthen the weary, and remind readers that God does not abandon His creation. He pursues them.

Brad continues to write devotionals, spiritual reflections, and stories for those who feel unseen or unheard. His prayer is that every reader will discover the power of speaking the name of Jesus and find stability in a world that often offers none.
