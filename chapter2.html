Chapter Two

The Valley of Temptation

Some valleys test your strength. Others reveal where strength truly comes from.

Dallas continued along the narrow trail as the night slowly gave way to dawn. The sky softened into shades of blue and lavender. The light should have comforted him, but instead a weight pressed into his chest. Every step deeper into the hills felt like stepping away from everything he had ever known, even the parts that had once harmed him.

His body ached. His hands trembled. Thoughts crashed through his mind in relentless waves. He recognized the signs. Withdrawal came in layers: first the anxiety, then the restlessness, then the physical ache that made his bones feel as if they were unraveling.

Dallas rubbed his arms and whispered: "I am trying, Lord. Please help me keep moving."

The trail opened into a valley surrounded by tall trees leaning inward, their branches heavy with night’s dew. The air felt thick, almost aware, as if creation itself recognized the battle taking place inside him.
