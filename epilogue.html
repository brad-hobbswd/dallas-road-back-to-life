Beyond the Mountain

Epilogue

Years after the night that changed him, Dallas stood on the hill overlooking the town where his darkest memories lived. He did not stand there as a man ashamed of his past. He stood as a living testimony of what Jesus can do.

The path he once walked was filled with addiction, confusion, and double-minded choices. Yet the Lord had rewritten his future with compassion stronger than rebellion.

He remembered the man he used to be. The man who fought secret battles. The man who prayed for deliverance while returning to the things that destroyed him. The man who feared God had grown tired of weakness.

Jesus never withdrew His hand. He reached for Dallas repeatedly until the day Dallas said yes to freedom without turning back.
