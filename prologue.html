The Journey Begins

Prologue

Dallas never imagined that life could change in a single moment. He had been a man torn between two worlds. One offered the promise of peace and wholeness, the voice of Jesus calling him out of darkness. The other offered temporary escapes that only deepened the ache inside him. For years he walked a thin line between hope and destruction, longing for freedom while running back to the very chains that held him captive.

He knew Scripture and believed in the power of God. Yet when cravings came and loneliness pressed against his mind, he felt drawn toward the poison that numbed him. He lived the words he had hoped no one would ever see. A double-minded man is unstable in all his ways. A verse had become a mirror he avoided.

Jesus never released His hold on Dallas. Through every relapse, every night spent trembling, and every moment he thought survival would slip away, the Lord watched over him with love greater than failure. In the middle of his darkest valley, Dallas discovered something he had never fully understood. Deliverance is not found in human strength. Deliverance is found in surrender to the God whose name is Jesus.

This story begins on the day Dallas realized that the life he had tried to manage alone must be placed completely into the hands of the One who formed him. His journey is not the story of a perfect man. His journey is the story of a pursued man, a rescued man, and a transformed man. Stability is not created within the human heart. Stability is created by Jesus living inside the heart that welcomes Him.
