Before the Journey

Preface

Life often places us at crossroads where choices are difficult, and the voices of the past and present pull us in different directions. This book tells the story of one man’s journey from addiction, confusion, and double mindedness to freedom, faith, and purpose. It is a story of struggle, surrender, and the power of trusting God even when the way forward seems uncertain.

As you read, you will walk with Dallas through dark streets, valleys of temptation, rivers of relapse, and mountains of freedom. You will witness his struggles, his victories, and ultimately the transformation that comes from a heart fully surrendered to God. This story is not only about one man but also reflects the battles many face in their own lives. My hope is that his journey will inspire, encourage, and remind you that no path is too difficult when you walk with faith.
