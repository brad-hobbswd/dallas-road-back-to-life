A journey through darkness toward freedom

DALLAS

and the Road Back to Life
Some roads lead deeper into darkness. Some roads lead home. Dallas spent years trapped between addiction, fear, shame, and the relentless battle within his own heart. Every street carried memories. Every choice pulled him toward two worlds. One voice offered temporary escape. Another called him toward healing, purpose, and freedom. Walk beside Dallas through valleys of temptation, rivers of relapse, mountains of surrender, and moments where grace reaches farther than failure. The journey begins with one step.
