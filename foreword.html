Preparing the Heart

Foreword

It is a rare gift to encounter a story that offers more than entertainment. This book reaches into the heart and soul, exploring the very real struggles of addiction, doubt, and spiritual conflict. The journey of Dallas reflects the human experience, with moments of weakness, the temptation to give in, and the longing for something greater.

What makes this story remarkable is not only the battles Dallas faces but also the lessons he learns along the way. It demonstrates that true strength is not found in human effort alone but in surrender, trust, and faith in God. Readers will find encouragement in his courage, hope in his perseverance, and inspiration in his ultimate triumph. This is a book for anyone seeking freedom from the chains of the past and a heart ready to embrace a life of purpose, love, and peace.
