Final Thoughts

Conclusion

There are seasons when shame, rejection, and past wounds convince you that you are unworthy to speak the name of Jesus. Yet He never stops calling and never stops reaching for the place in you that feels disqualified. His Spirit still chooses those who were once overlooked.

This book is a journey of healing for anyone wounded by the very place that should have provided shelter. This story speaks to those who grew up in church homes or ministry families where expectations felt heavy while compassion often felt absent.

Through personal reflections, vivid storytelling, and Scripture rooted in the Oneness revelation, you will witness the process of restoration and the power of a God who steps into broken places and raises voices others tried to silence.

Dallas's journey serves as a reminder that freedom rarely arrives in a single moment. Freedom often unfolds one surrendered step at a time. Valleys, rivers, mountains, and crossroads become places where grace teaches the heart to trust again.

This story is a call to rise again, speak again, and believe again. Jesus is still the God who heals, restores, and raises His chosen for such a time as this.
